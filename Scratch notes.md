# **Curriculum Ideas, Questions & Possible Direction**

## **Core Problem**

Traditional composition teaching often starts with:

- Harmony
- Counterpoint
- Form
- Orchestration

Students learn techniques but often still struggle to answer:

- How do I start a piece?
- How do I write a melody?
- Why choose one idea over another?
- What do I do when faced with a blank page?

Alex’s philosophy seems to address this by starting from:
Experience
↓
Image
↓
Gesture
↓
Motive
↓
Development
↓
Form
↓
Composition

The question becomes:

  

How do we turn this philosophy into practical projects and exercises?
